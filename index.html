Space Math

Instructions:
You have gained control of an alien spacecraft. There's only one problem: the aliens are math fanatics
and their spacecraft can only be controlled using math equations... Adjust the parameters of the math equation
in the console using the arrow buttons and maneuver the spacecraft to gather the alien energy balls which, luckily,
are also laid out in the path of math equations!
The x exponent parameter ranges from 0 to 4; other parameters range -3 to 3.
Click "GO" and your spinning spacecraft will travel along the equation graph collecting the black & light blue energy balls.
For your first hundred energy balls, the equation will be plotted in red in the console window. After that you
will enter advanced mode and the plot will not be displayed.
